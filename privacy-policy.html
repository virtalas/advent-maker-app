WishKit Feedback Framework

This app uses WishKit, a third-party feedback and feature-request service provided by WishKit UG. When you submit feedback, feature requests, or interact with the WishKit interface within the app, the following information may be collected and processed by WishKit:

  • The feedback or feature request content you submit
  • Optional information you choose to provide (such as email or name), if requested by the app
  • Technical information such as IP address, device details, and usage data automatically collected by WishKit's servers

This information is transmitted directly to WishKit and handled according to WishKit’s own privacy policy. We do not control or have access to WishKit’s internal data processing.

For more information, please review WishKit’s privacy policy:
https://www.wishkit.io/privacy-policy

This information is collected and processed directly by Google in accordance with its own privacy policy.

For more information, please visit Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
